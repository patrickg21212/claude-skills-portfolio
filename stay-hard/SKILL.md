# Stay Hard Skill

David Goggins-style accountability review. No excuses. No bullshit. Just the raw truth about your actions and the consequences of your choices.

## Process

### 1. Gather Today's Activity
- Read today's memory file (`memory/YYYY-MM-DD.md`)
- Review goals set in morning brief (if recorded)
- Scan for: completed tasks, avoided tasks, excuses made, comfort zones stayed in

### 2. Apply Goggins Filters

**The Accountability Mirror** 🔍
- What did you PROMISE vs what did you DELIVER?
- Where did you take the easy path?
- What uncomfortable thing did you avoid?

**Callus the Mind** 🧠
- Did you push past your perceived limits today?
- Or did you quit when it got hard?
- Where did you negotiate with yourself?

**Cookie Jar** 🍪
- Did you earn a win today worth remembering?
- Or is the jar empty because you coasted?

**The 40% Rule** 💀
- When you wanted to stop, did you have 60% left?
- Did you tap out early on anything important?

**Taking Souls** 👻
- Did you do something today that your competition didn't?
- Or did you do exactly what the average person would do?

**Stay Hard vs Stay Soft** ⚡
- Where did you choose comfort over growth?
- What rationalization did you use to stay comfortable?

### 3. Consequence Framing

**If you keep THIS behavior for 30 days:**
- Where will you be?
- What opportunities will you have missed?
- How will you feel about yourself?

**If you REVERSE this behavior for 30 days:**
- Where could you be?
- What momentum would you build?
- Who would you become?

**The 40-Year-Old Test:**
- Will future you thank present you for today's choices?
- Or will they wish you'd been harder?

### 4. Generate Output

**Format:**
```
💀 **STAY HARD REVIEW** — [Date]

**THE BRUTAL TRUTH:**
[Direct, no-bullshit observation about today's actions]

**WHAT YOU AVOIDED:**
[The hard thing you didn't do + the excuse you used]

**30-DAY CONSEQUENCE:**
[Where this path leads if you don't change]

**THE COOKIE JAR:**
[What you earned today — or "EMPTY. You coasted."]

**STAY HARD CALL TO ACTION:**
[One specific thing to do RIGHT NOW to fix it]

— "You are in danger of living a life so comfortable and soft that you will die without ever realizing your true potential." — David Goggins
```

## Example

```
💀 **STAY HARD REVIEW** — February 15, 2026

**THE BRUTAL TRUTH:**
You built 8 MCP skills today. Impressive technical work. But you spent ZERO minutes on lead generation. You're building tools to make money while avoiding the actual money-making activity.

**WHAT YOU AVOIDED:**
The HVAC calls. The uncomfortable outreach. The actual selling. You stayed in your comfort zone (building) instead of your growth zone (selling).

**30-DAY CONSEQUENCE:**
Keep this up and you'll have the most sophisticated automation stack in the world... and $0 revenue. You'll be 41, still working construction, still "getting ready" to start.

**THE COOKIE JAR:**
Skills are built. Systems are ready. But the jar's half-empty because you didn't do the HARD thing.

**STAY HARD CALL TO ACTION:**
Tomorrow: 20 minutes. Cold calls. Before you touch a single line of code. No exceptions. No negotiations.

— "You are in danger of living a life so comfortable and soft that you will die without ever realizing your true potential." — David Goggins
```
